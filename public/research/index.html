Research

We share anonymized gameplay snapshots with the community. These records are designed to be useful for AI research and data analysis while protecting player privacy.

  • Open and free. Anyone can explore, learn, and build with the data.
  • Privacy‑first. No accounts, no PII, no persistent identity across runs.
  • Made for research. Structured to support reproducible experiments and model training.
  • Always improving. We iterate on schema and docs to keep the dataset best‑in‑class.
